FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about fertility treatments.

In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) is an advanced fertility treatment in which eggs from the woman and sperm from the man are fertilized in a specialized laboratory. After fertilization, the healthiest embryo is carefully transferred into the uterus to support pregnancy.
IVF may be recommended for couples facing fertility challenges such as poor sperm quality, damaged fallopian tubes, age-related fertility decline, or hormonal imbalance. It offers another path toward parenthood when natural conception or simpler treatments have not succeeded.
Yes, IVF can also support advanced fertility options including donor egg, donor sperm, surrogacy, and preimplantation genetic testing (PGT), depending on individual treatment requirements. Modern IVF technology and personalized treatment planning have significantly improved the chances of successful pregnancy.
Step-By-Step Process

Procedure for IVF Treatment at Janya Fertility

At Janya Fertility, our experienced IVF specialists follow a step-by-step process to provide safe, personalized, and compassionate fertility treatment.

Step 1

Consultation & Evaluation

  • Medical history review
  • Previous fertility treatments review
  • Hormone blood tests
  • Ovarian reserve assessment
  • Pelvic ultrasound scan
  • Semen analysis
  • Hysteroscopy or genetic screening (when required)
Step 2

Ovarian Stimulation

  • Hormonal injections for 8–14 days
  • Regular ultrasound monitoring
  • Hormone level tracking
  • Multiple healthy follicle development
Step 3

Egg Collection

  • HCG Trigger shot administration
  • Egg retrieval after 34–36 hours
  • Ultrasound-guided precision procedure
  • Light sedation for complete comfort
  • Quick 20–30 minute procedure
Step 4

Sperm Collection & Processing

  • Fresh semen sample collection
  • Specialized healthy sperm selection
  • Advanced PESA procedure (when needed)
  • Advanced TESE procedure (when needed)
Step 5

Fertilization & Embryo Growth

  • Standard IVF fertilization
  • Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI)
  • Embryo culture in advanced incubators
  • Continuous 3–5 day monitoring
Step 6

Embryo Transfer

  • High-quality embryo selection
  • Painless, gentle transfer into uterus
  • Cryopreservation of remaining embryos
Step 7

Hormonal Support

  • Tailored progesterone supplementation
  • Uterine lining support
  • Ongoing care & monitoring
Step 8

Pregnancy Testing

  • Beta-hCG blood test after 12–14 days
  • Early confirmation ultrasound
  • Next phase treatment & prenatal guidance
